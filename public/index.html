A Message From the VentureGuard Team

"After a long life operating businesses in and around Australia, we decided it was time to give back."

Between us, we have built, scaled, and — yes — sometimes failed at businesses across nearly every industry this country has to offer. We made expensive mistakes so you don't have to. We learned what truly matters when you are starting out, and what most advice gets wrong.

This questionnaire was built from the ground up based on what we genuinely believe is important for people trying to start their own business — and for people who want honest feedback rather than empty encouragement. Every question has been chosen because we've seen its impact firsthand.

Not all questions carry equal weight: some factors are simply more critical to your success than others, and our scoring formula reflects that. Financial readiness and lease obligations, for example, are weighted more heavily because we know from experience that these are the areas most likely to determine whether your business survives its first two years.

We believe this approach is more valuable than anything else currently available to aspiring business owners in Australia. The feedback we've already received from people who have completed the assessment confirms it — people telling us it gave them clarity and the courage to either move forward with confidence, or pause and better prepare.

This is our way of giving back. We want your journey to begin on the best possible footing.

— The VentureGuard Team, Australia
